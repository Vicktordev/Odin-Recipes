Indomie Noodles

Description

The indomie noodles is an awesome meal and perfect for days when you might need a big bowl of comfort. Luckily, its also incredibly quick and easy to make, with all the ingredients simply tossed into one pot and cooked until Al dente!

Ingredients

  • 2 Packs of Indomie noodles
  • 2 glasses of water
  • 3 small red pepper
  • 1 small ball of onions
  • Preferred meat source (Eggs or chicken are the most widely used)
  • 3 sausages
  • Green beans and carrot( Optional but great for beauty)
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Start by par boiling for about 3 mins
  2. Then you take it and drain it into a seive
  3. Next put some vegetable oil in a pan under medium heat
  4. Pour the noodles into the hot oil,add all your spices and mixed veggies and stir occasionally so it does'nt get burnt
  5. Next add your eggs( already whisked) into the frying indomie
  6. Stir-fry for few minutes until its done.
