Nigerian beef suya

Description

Nigerian Suya Recipe! This beef suya is a popular street food in Nigeria,West Africa. It is flavourful,nutty,meaty,smokey and so delicious. This homemadesuya recipeis easy,simple and straightfoward and a must try if you are looking for a trusted recipe. Let me show you how to make suya from scratch

Ingredients

  • 500g (1lb) beef
  • 3 tablespoon suya spice halved
  • 1 5g stock cube
  • Salt
  • 2 tablespoon oil

To serve

  • 1 tablespoon suya spice
  • 1 medium onion- Red or browm sized
  • 2 tomatoes cut
  • 1 cucumber sliced

Steps

  1. Cut the steak across the grain into the strips. Add stock cube,half of the suya spiceand salt to the beef strips. Mix well, cover with clingfilm and leave in fridge to marinate for at least 30 mins
  2. Bring out the marinated beef and thread on pre-soaked skewers. Apply the remaining halfof the suya spice all over then brush with oil.
  3. Arrange the skewered beefon an oven rack then grill in the oven at a temperature of 175C(fan) for 15 mins. Turn the beef to the other side and grill for another 10 mins.
  4. Bring out of the oven, remove suya from skewers, sprinkle with more suya spice, and then serve with sliced onions,tomatoes,cucumbers. You can also add cabbage. Enjoy your delicious,nutty,tasty,smokey Nigerian suya
