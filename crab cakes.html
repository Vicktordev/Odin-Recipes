Crab Cake

Description

Crab cakesare always in the appetizers sections of fancy seadood restuarants, so people are often intimidated to make them at home. DONT BE. They are so easy literally anyone can do it. Here's everything you need to know.

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of mayonnaise
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoon Dijion mustard
  • 2 tablespoon worcestershire sause
  • 1/2 tablespoon hotsause
  • Freahly ground black pepper
  • 1 lb jumbo lump crabmeat,picked over for shells
    • Canola oil, for frying
  • Tartar sauce, for serving
  • 2 tablespoon freshly chopped paresly
  • 3/4 cups panko bread crumbs

Steps

  1. In a small bowl,whisk together mayo,egg, Dijion mustard,worcestershire and hot sause and season with salt and pepper.
  2. In a medium bowl, stir together stir together crabmeat, panko and paresly. Fold in mayo mixture, then form into 8 patties
  3. In a large skillet over medium-high heat ,coat pan with oil and haet until shimmering. Add crabcakes and cook in batches until golden and crispy, 3 to 5 minutes per side
  4. Pour the noodles into the hot oil,add all your spices and mixed veggies and stir occasionally so it does'nt get burnt
  5. serve with lemon and Tartar sauce
